I have always been drawn to both logic and creativity.
There is something comforting about knowing that this will result in that, and any problem can be solved if you just have the correct tools and know how to use them. I have enjoyed a past career in forensic science, where I applied critical thinking to solve mysteries and do some social good.
There is something freeing in knowing you can create something new, something different in some way from anything else in existence. This love of creation has fueled art projects galore, projects of baking, knitting, sewing, and performance.
The desire to combine what I love from science and from art have led me to where I am now: web development. As a developer, I aim to draw on the skills I've gained, never stop learning, and create a positive impact for those around me.
